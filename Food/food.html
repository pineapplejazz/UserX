Feeling hungry? Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Local Cuisine

Tiki Breeze Grill

4342 W Taniti Dr, Taniti
×
×

Experience local cuisine at Tiki Breeze Grill. Tiki Breeze Grill offers delicious island food for everyone to enjoy. It offers a beautiful view of the ocean since it is located directly on the beach. This restaurant offers both indoor and outdoor seating and is dedicated to serving delicious meals to enjoy with others!

Rating: ★★★★★

Lagoon and Lime Bistro

1111 E Taniti Dr, Taniti

Lagoon and Lime Bistro is a family-owned and operated Tanitian restaurant located at the heart of Taniti. Enjoy authentic fish and rice while supporting a local community business.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Sunset Drift Shack

5050 SW Taniti Ave, Taniti

Come to Sunset Drift Shack. Famed for its award of being one of the world’s best late-night dining. It features glass architecture, high-backed booths, and ship-themed chairs. It is a two-story restaurant located in the center of downtown Taniti

Rating: ★★★★★

Coral Cove Bites

9520 W Palm Taniti Dr, Taniti

For more than 20 years, Coral Cove Bites has been serving up high-quality fresh seafood cooked to perfection. Come relax and dine at Coral Cove Bites. It is located on the outskirts of downtown, making it the perfect local place to bring family and friends.

Rating: ★★★★★

Palm Haven Eatery

1515 SW Grand Taniti Dr, Taniti

Introducing traditional Taniti food to locals and tourists, Palm Haven eatery serves up family recipes. Reserve your table at Palm Haven Eatery and discover your next favorite culinary destination.

Rating: ★★★★☆

American-Style

Eagle’s Burgers & Steak

1010 N Grand Taniti St, Taniti

Introducing classic American food to Taniti, Eagle’s Burgers & Steak serves delicious meals, such as cheeseburgers and grilled steak. Located near the Tahiti airport, this is the perfect place for tourists who have just arrived in Taniti. This restaurant features paintings of Taniti as indoor decor.

Rating: ★★★☆☆

Rustic Fork & Barrel

1515 NE Grand Taniti Dr, Taniti

Rustic Fork & Barrel offers an exciting and modern dining experience. Rustic Fork & Barrel reflects the waves of the Taniti Beach and the changing tides of the Palm Beach. The restaurant’s scenic location provides a welcoming atmosphere with a pretty backdrop. In addition, customers can expect to experience incredible service and a great ambience. Rustic Fork & Barrel also has a bar with delicious drinks to enjoy!

Rating: ★★★★★

Hometown Grillery

1599 N Little Taniti St, Taniti

With its comfy atmosphere and great selection of food, Hometown Grillery attracts both locals and tourists. Hometown Grillery makes everything from scratch and roasts their own meat.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Pan-Asian Cuisine

Sakura Sushi

7200 N Little Palm Taniti St, Taniti

As the only revolving sushi bar in Taniti, it is a local favorite. Come and enjoy the modern dining experience with robot servers. Sakura Sushi features fresh, high-quality seafood prepped as sushi and sashimi.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Kurin Thai

7202 N Little Palm Taniti St, Taniti

Kurin Thai offers delicious, authentic Thai food. This restaurant features a warm atmosphere with paintings of Thailand. Kurin Thai is located on the eastern part of the island, right next to Sakura Sushi.

Rating: ★★★★★

Grocery

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.
