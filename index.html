About the Island

Taniti is a little island that is located in the Pacific. The island is a tropical island with an area that’s less than 500 square miles. Its terrain varies and includes both sandy and rocky beaches. In addition, it features a small but safe harbor, pretty tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano. Taniti’s indigenous population is about 20,000. Then, until the recent increase in tourism, the Tanitian economy was dependent and dominated by fishing and agriculture. There are plenty of things to do and sights to see in Taniti. Make Taniti your next adventure!
